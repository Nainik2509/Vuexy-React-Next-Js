<p>On this page we will explain the usage of Redux in Vuexy React</p>

### Overview

---

<p>
  Our template uses <a href='https://redux-toolkit.js.org/'>redux-toolkit</a> to bind the react with redux. Store
  configurations are located in <code>@src/redux/store.js</code>.
</p>

<p>
  The globally used <code>slices</code> are in <code>@src/redux</code>.
</p>

<p>
  The Component specific <code>slices</code> are located in the <code>@src/view/</code> folder. Refer{' '}
  <code>@src/views/apps/email/store/index.js</code> as reference.
</p>
